Cozy Evenings
The Vibe

An Intimate Escape

When the sun sets, the day's chatter gives way to the gentle hum of acoustic music, clinking crystal glasses, and warm, soft candlelight. The Drawing Room by Evening is designed as a sophisticated yet deeply cozy hideaway to decompress after a busy week.

We showcase a curated list of artisanal spirits, signature cocktails crafted with fresh botanicals, and hand-selected small-producer wines. Relax on our velvet sofas and enjoy a grazing experience created to be savoured slowly.
