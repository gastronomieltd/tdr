Welcome to Our Evening Sanctuary

Relaxing Dine & Wine

Nestled in the heart of Locksbottom, The Drawing Room is a charming haven that radiates warmth and elegant simplicity. The Drawing Room by Evening is designed as a sophisticated yet deeply cosy hideaway to decompress after a busy day.

Our dedicated team is committed to serving heartwarming artisan food with wide range of wines and spirits.

Indulge in artisan pizzas, tapas, and handcrafted charcuterie and cheese boards.
