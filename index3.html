Welcome

Located at 405 Crofton Road, The Drawing Room is a traditional British tearoom and café known for warm hospitality, homemade food, afternoon tea and our famous Hot Salt Beef on Rye.

Our Specialities

Afternoon Tea

Finger sandwiches, homemade cakes, fruit scones, clotted cream and unlimited tea.

Hot Salt Beef

Our signature dish served on rye bread with dill pickle, mustard and homemade coleslaw.

Homemade Cakes

Fresh cakes and traybakes prepared daily.

Artisan Coffee

Freshly brewed coffee and speciality teas throughout the day.

Afternoon Tea Experience

Perfect for birthdays, celebrations and special occasions.
