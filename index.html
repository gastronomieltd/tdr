Welcome

Located at 405 Crofton Road, The Drawing Room is a traditional British tearoom and café known for warm hospitality, homemade food, afternoon tea and our famous Hot Salt Beef on Rye.

Our Specialities

🫖 Traditional Afternoon Tea

Finger sandwiches, homemade cakes, fruit scones, clotted cream and unlimited tea served on elegant vintage china.

🥪 Famous Hot Salt Beef

Our signature dish served on rye bread with dill pickle, mustard and homemade coleslaw.

🍰 Homemade Cakes

A daily selection of cakes, traybakes and sweet treats including vegan and gluten-free options.

☕ Artisan Coffee

Freshly brewed coffee and speciality teas served throughout the day.
