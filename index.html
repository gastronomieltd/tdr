Our Story

Where Nostalgia Meets Hospitality

Nestled in the heart of Locksbottom, The Drawing Room is a charming haven that radiates warmth and elegant simplicity. Upon stepping inside, you are greeted by cozy vintage decor, fresh blooms, and beautiful mismatched teacups that set the stage for an unforgettable visit.

Our dedicated team is committed to delivering heartwarming, traditional English cuisine infused with quality and flair. From our hearty lunches to the delicate pastries served during our signature afternoon tea, everything we present is crafted with care.
