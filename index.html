Welcome to Our Sanctuary

Vintage Charm & Modern Delights

Nestled in the heart of Locksbottom, The Drawing Room is a charming haven that radiates warmth and elegant simplicity. Upon stepping inside, you are greeted by cosy vintage decor, fresh blooms, and beautiful mismatched teacups that set the stage for an unforgettable visit.

Our dedicated team is committed to delivering heartwarming, traditional English cuisine infused with quality and flair. From our hearty lunches to the delicate pastries, crafted with care and served with love.

Curated Favourites

Our House Specialties

The Drawing Room experiences are quintessentially handcrafted daily and designed to delight your senses.

Elegant Tradition

Afternoon Tea

Delicate finger sandwiches, freshly baked warm scones served with Cornish clotted cream, and an exquisite tier of miniature pastries paired with your choice of tea.

Savoury Classic

Signature Salt Beef

Slow-braised, melt-in-the-mouth salt beef stacked high on freshly cut, thick-sliced bloomer bread. Accompanied by traditional pickle wedges and hot English mustard.

Baked Fresh

Victoria Sponge

Our masterfully light, double-layered sponge filled generously with rich vanilla buttercream and deep, sweet home-style raspberry preserve, dusted with powdered sugar.
