Welcome to The Drawing Room

Located at 405 Crofton Road in Locksbottom, The Drawing Room is a traditional British tearoom and café known for its warm hospitality, homemade food and vintage charm.


Whether you're joining us for breakfast, lunch, our famous Hot Salt Beef on Rye, or a beautifully presented Afternoon Tea, every visit is designed to be relaxed, welcoming and memorable.

Our Specialities

🫖 Traditional Afternoon Tea

Finger sandwiches, homemade cakes, fruit scones, clotted cream and unlimited tea served on elegant vintage china.

🥪 Famous Hot Salt Beef

Our signature dish served on rye bread with dill pickle, mustard and homemade coleslaw.

🍰 Homemade Cakes

A daily selection of cakes, traybakes and sweet treats including vegan and gluten-free options.

☕ Artisan Coffee

Freshly brewed coffee and speciality teas served throughout the day.

Afternoon Tea Experience

Freshly prepared finger sandwiches, homemade fruit scones, clotted cream, strawberry preserve, cakes and unlimited tea.
